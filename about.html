Introduction:

This is a static web app that makes it easy to deal with data , instead of the regular way with pen and pencil and a copybook you just type name of product, buying price and selling price,and the app will calculate the p&l(profit or loss) for you automatically.

It also insert all the data automatically to it corespondant place with just a click of button and export it as excel file.

Features:

  1. Insert data dynamically into the table.
  2. Calculates the price automatically.
  3. Edit data easy and smooth.
  4. Calculate everything with just a click.
  5. Export the data as excel file.
