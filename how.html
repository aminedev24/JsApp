How to use:

You need to do the following:

  1. Write your product name or just choose from the dropdown.
  2. Write Buying price of the product.
  3. Write your selling price of the product.
  4. Click the button or just press enter.

If you want to edit the data you just have to hover on it and click the Edit button.

Once you finished writing all the data click the Sum button to calculate all the data, Then extract the data as an excel file by clicking the Export button.

Notice:

The application is still currentely in development many bugs and errors can be experienced.
